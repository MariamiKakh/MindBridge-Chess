# Experiment Plan

The experiment flow uses three stimulus stages:

1. Piece flash sequence: all player pieces flash one by one while EEG is monitored for P300 responses.
2. Direction flash groups: eight direction groups flash simultaneously, and the selected direction is decoded.
3. Square flash sequence: squares along the chosen direction flash sequentially until the destination is selected.

Timing, trigger codes, and classifier parameters are stored in `configs/experiment.yaml`.
